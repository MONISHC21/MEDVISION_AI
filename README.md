🩺 MedVision AI

«Transforming Complex Medical Reports into Clear, Actionable Health Insights with AI»

MedVision AI is an AI-powered healthcare assistant that helps users understand medical reports, prescriptions, and laboratory results through intelligent analysis, patient-friendly explanations, and personalized wellness recommendations.

Instead of struggling with complex medical terminology, users can upload their reports and receive easy-to-understand explanations, health summaries, normal value comparisons, trend analysis, and AI-guided educational insights.

✨ Features

- 📄 AI-powered Medical Report Analysis
- 🩸 Blood Test Interpretation
- 💊 Prescription & Medication Explanation
- 🧠 Medical Term Explainer
- 📊 Normal Range Comparison
- 📈 Previous vs Current Report Comparison
- 📉 Health Trend Tracking
- ❤️ Personalized Health Score
- 🥗 Nutrition Recommendations
- 🏃 Exercise & Lifestyle Guidance
- 💬 AI Health Assistant Chatbot
- 🌍 Multilingual Support
- 📥 Downloadable Professional Health Reports
- 👶 "Explain Like I'm 10" Mode
- 🔒 Secure Authentication & Cloud Storage

---

🚀 How It Works

1. Upload a medical report (PDF or image).
2. OCR extracts medical information.
3. AI analyzes laboratory values and prescriptions.
4. Compare results with reference ranges.
5. Generate easy-to-understand explanations.
6. Provide educational health insights.
7. Recommend nutrition, exercise, and lifestyle improvements.
8. Compare current and previous reports.
9. Download a professional AI-generated health report.
